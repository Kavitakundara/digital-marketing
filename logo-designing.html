Elements Of Logo Design

We now have a clear understanding of what logo design entails. Now, let's look at the elements that make up a logo design

Typography

Typography design is the art of organising a message in textual and visually appealing composition. It is used to create your company's brand name and tagline.

The Image

It is entirely up to the customer whether they require a text logo or a picture logo. A combination of the two will greatly aid in easy brand recognition.

Colour

Using different colours can help you communicate in a variety of ways. The blue colour represents finance, while the green colour represents organic products.
